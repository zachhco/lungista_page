HERE'S HOW YOU CAN HELP!

Donations allow us to fund our food supply and reach more of our community. We appreciate donations of any size and thank you for your thoughtfulness. If you have any questions about other ways to get involved, please get in touch with us via our contact form.






Mission

Our mission is to eradicate poverty in the community at large. We aim to provide food security in vulnerable underprivileged, and impoverished communities.

Nutrition

We work with our volunteer kitchens to provide food to seniors and to youth before and after school.

Relief

Providing nutrition allows our beneficiaries to live a fuller life where they don't have to worry about access to food.

Empowerment

Empowerment means more than just the provision of food. We provide skill workshops for the unemployed within our communities and give youth access to various recreational and creative opportunities.
